How NovaX works

From order booking to seller payment without blind spots.

01Seller books one order or uploads bulk orders.
02Rider pickup is scanned before parcel enters branch custody.
03Warehouse scan and sack manifest prevent parcel leakage.
04Destination branch receives, ages, and assigns delivery.
05Rider delivery proof unlocks COD collection.
06Invoice and payout appear in seller account.
